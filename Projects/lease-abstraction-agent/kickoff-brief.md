# Lease Abstraction Agent — Product discovery & kickoff (Saquib)

Prep for kickoff sync. See also [[PRD]].

## What is this?

**Lease Abstraction Agent** — an agentic product that reads lease agreements, turns them into **structured obligations** (dates, deliverables, proof), then **runs the loop**: reminders, follow-ups, and (over time) verification and filing — mostly over **email/text** so **tenants don’t need a login**.

---

## Why is it a problem worth solving?

- **Operational reality:** Large-property landlords juggle many tenants and non-standard leases. Obligations (insurance, maintenance, notices) are easy to **miss or enforce late** → **risk, disputes, and direct money left on the table**.
- **Scale:** Manual abstraction and spreadsheet tracking **don’t scale** with lease format variance.
- **Strategic fit (Oak):** Current company bet to prove **AI + agents** can ship real value while **finding PMF** — so it is both a **landlord operations** problem and a **company learning** problem.

---

## Is it an actual problem?

Treat as a **hypothesis**, not settled:

- **Strong intuition:** Lease compliance and renewal obligations are a **known pain** in property operations.
- **Still to prove:** **Quantified pain** (frequency, $ at stake) and **willingness to pay / adopt** from a **named design partner**. Strategy assumes **validation through the lease agent**, not a fixed product direction yet.

**Kickoff should lock how you’ll prove it** (pilot, metrics, timeline).

---

## Who is it for?

| Segment | Role |
|--------|------|
| **Primary** | **Landlords / asset managers** of large, multi-tenant properties (malls, data centers, large residential) — upload leases, see obligations, own enforcement. |
| **Secondary** | **Tenants** — outbound **email/text** only; **no product login** in the PRD vision. |
| **Early commercial hook** | **Karma Automotive** / **$100M sales volume** appear elsewhere in Oak goals — **clarify with Saquib** whether Karma is **first design partner**, **distribution**, or a **separate track** so expectations don’t blur. |

## What is the goal?

- **Primary (aligned with Saquib + [[PRD]]):** **speed of execution** — a **thin slice live fast**, learn, then iterate; scope and polish follow traction.
- **Product:** **Get live and gain traction** — a system that **abstracts → persists → reminds/enforces** on real leases, not a slide deck.
- **Company:** **Revenue as north star**; use the agent to **test PMF** and prove Oak can **ship AI that matters**.

Make “live” concrete: **pilot customer**, **first success metric** (e.g. leases processed, obligations extracted, reminders sent, time-to-compliance), and **target date for “live.”**
